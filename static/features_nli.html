Multi-Model NLI Verification

Ensuring factual consistency by mathematically comparing claims against verified evidence.

Why This Matters

Large Language Models (LLMs) often generate "stable hallucinations"—statements that sound extremely confident and plausible but are factually incorrect. Traditional keyword matching fails here. NLI (Natural Language Inference) allows us to mathematically verify if a generated claim is actually supported by the evidence.
