About Hallucination Risk

This score represents the probability that an AI-generated response contains unsupported, contradictory, or fabricated information — commonly known as AI hallucination.

Risk Calculation Factors
  • NLI contradiction score
  • Grounding search mismatch
  • Cross-model disagreement
  • Heatmap uncertainty analysis
Interpretation Guide
Low (0–30%) — Mostly factual, well-grounded response
Medium (31–70%) — Some uncertainty or unsupported claims
High (71–100%) — Potential hallucination detected
