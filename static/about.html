About TruthLens AI

Built to Detect What Others Miss

TruthLens AI is a multi-signal hallucination detection system that evaluates LLM-generated responses across five independent signals — giving you confidence in every AI answer.

Our Mission

As LLMs become integral to research, healthcare, and enterprise, the cost of hallucination is immense. TruthLens gives developers a transparent, multi-signal view of LLM trustworthiness — making AI more accountable.

How It Works

Our pipeline accepts a user prompt and an LLM response, then runs it through five independent detection modules. Each module contributes a risk score, fused into an overall hallucination risk rating.

Open Architecture

Built with a modular, pluggable design — swap out models, add new signals, or extend the pipeline. FastAPI backend + vanilla JS frontend keeps the stack light and hackable.

About TruthLens AI

Advancing the frontiers of LLM reliability and factual integrity.

Our Mission

TruthLens was founded with a single goal: to solve the "hallucination problem" in generative AI. As LLMs become integrated into critical workflows, the need for automated, mathematical verification of their outputs has never been greater.

Our system uses a multi-layered diagnostic pipeline that combines Natural Language Inference (NLI), real-time web grounding, and token-level uncertainty analysis to provide a transparent reliability score for every claim.

Technology

Built on top of state-of-the-art NLP models like DeBERTa-v3 and Sentence Transformers, TruthLens leverages vector search and real-time RAG to verify facts against the live web.

Research Driven

Inspired by academic benchmarks like HaluEval and SelfCheckGPT, we implement the latest research in self-consistency and cross-model verification.
